About Us

It’s time to change “HOW THE WORLD VIEWS ELECTRONICS”
- By making devices affordable and more earth-friendly.

At Btech Market, we are committed to making high-grade technology available to everyone who needs it. At the same time, we prevent reusable laptops, phones, PCs, and other devices from being discarded and ending up as e-waste. We hope that our refurbished electronics will make a difference in the community by reaching potentially-talented people who can turn the world around, but don’t have access to AFFORDABLE technology to make their dreams a reality.

How do you purchase new electronics without worrying about the environmental impact?

If you always thought using electronics saves trees and lowers the usage of paper, know that the reverse is actually true. Manufacturing and developing electronic devices has a serious impact on the socio-economic conditions and environment of the country. Each time a microwave, tablet, smartphone, or laptop leaves the factory, it will eventually add to the mounds of toxic e-waste that causes long-term damage to the earth.

Our Solution?

It’s simple, really. We refurbish THEM. We accept old and used devices and take them apart. After checking each device and its different components for functionality, we replace the old and worn-out parts with new ones. The refurbished phone, laptop, or PC finds a new home. And, a happy customer looking for AFFORDABLE electronics takes them home where they’ll be put to good use for as long as is possible.
