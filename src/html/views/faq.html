FAQs - Returning and Exchanging Products

If you aren’t quite happy with the product because of some technical issues, you must contact the refurbisher right away. Log into your account and you’ll find a button that says, “Contact the Vendor.” You’ll receive detailed instructions on how to return the products. bTech Market allows you up to 30 days to send back the device and request a refund for the entire price paid.
Before you sign the delivery receipt, and receive the product, make sure to check the external packaging. Contact the refurbisher within a 48-hour window so the company can make inquiries at the shipping agency.
In case the product is damaged like, for instance, the screen or camera is broken, even though the packaging is intact, contact the refurbisher. It is advisable that you make your request within 48 hours of receipt.

Get in touch with the refurbisher by logging in to your account on the website. You can use the contact page to clearly explain what is wrong with the product. Adding pictures to support your explanation of the defects helps. For instance, if the specifications are not working. Each detail you provide helps move the process along. You can also put down what the refurbishing company can do to right the error.

In case the refurbisher is at fault, you’ll receive a return slip so you can return the item. If a similar model is available, you’ll receive a replacement. If not, the company will send you a refund. Our platform monitors your communication and you can expect a fair settlement of the dispute.

Before sending back a rejected product, make sure to log out of your iCloud and Google accounts. In case you’re still logged in when the product reaches the refurbisher’s workshop, they’ll need you to send in the information so that the techs can process your complaint and provide repairs, replacements, or refunds on the order. Resolving your complaint can thus take longer if your device is locked to iCloud or Google accounts.

Contact the seller within 48 hours. Typically, vendors start an inquiry that takes a maximum of 5 to 7 days to resolve. Once the matter is sorted, you can expect to receive a full refund of the amount you paid. Alternatively, depending on the availability of stock, you’ll receive a replacement product similar to the one you ordered originally.

Log into your account on the bTech Market website. We have an order tracking feature that informs you about the current status of the order. If the vendor has not yet shipped the order, you can contact the seller and place your request for cancellation or change in the shipping address.

If the vendor has already shipped the order, the package will arrive at your original address. If it is not claimed, the shipping company will return it to the refurbisher in 10-15 days’ time. You should receive a refund, automatically. In any case, it helps to have an open customer service request on our site until the issue is resolved.

Do keep in mind that cancellation and changes in the shipping address can take some time to take effect. For this reason, there may be delays in processing your request depending on the size of the vendor’s operations.

FAQs - Warranty and Compatibility

When you purchase a product on bTech Market, you’ll receive a 1-year warranty from the date of purchase which is free of cost. This warranty provides assurance for malfunctions and defective parts. For instance, you can contact us in case the touch screen is not functioning or the battery is weak. Of course, the warranty does not apply in case of lost, dropped, or broken products. We also don’t cover oxidized devices which can happen if they are dropped in water.
If you have any problems with the products bought on our website, we encourage you to contact the seller or our customer service center. The warranty will no longer remain valid if the product is taken apart by any other person. Visit our site and let the seller know about the issues you’re facing. You can then return the device for further testing. If repairs cannot be made, you’ll receive a refund. Depending on availability, the vendor might choose to replace the product with another refurb.
ALL Refurbished phones are UNLOCKED which means that you can purchase a new SIM card and insert it into the handset. If you’re like more information about the compatibility of the phone with a specific telecom company, it is best that you check the website of the original manufacturer. Like, for instance, Samsung, Nokia, Apple, or any other. You could also check with your mobile network provider about the specific handsets they support and then finalize your purchase.
